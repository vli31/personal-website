Hi! I'm Victoria, a student at Harvard studying Computer Science and Statistics (with some Mind Brain & Behavior and Economics mixed in). I work on AI research at the Harvard Kempner Institute and Insight + Interaction Lab, where I'm fortunate to learn from and collaborate with wonderful people, including Martin Wattenberg, Naomi Saphra, Yonatan Belinkov, and David Alvarez-Melis.

I'm interested in interpretability and human-AI interaction. I'm also excited about intersections of AI with biology, health, and cognitive science.

Outside of research, I've spent time singing, teaching, supporting women in CS, and thinking about tech for global health and AI safety.
